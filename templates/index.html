Pipeline Flow Stages

Step 01

Ingestion Scan

The patient uploads a high-definition photograph of the skin lesion. Images are preprocessed and formatted to the network's required dimensions.

Step 02

Binary Categorization

The primary model evaluates visual pathology attributes to classify the lesion under broad "Skin Allergy" or "Skin Infection" groups.

Step 03

Specialist Inferences

The image is routed to a specialized second-stage model to diagnose exact pathology (e.g. Scabies, Eczema, or Tinea Corporis).

Step 04

Grad-CAM Explainability

Backpropagation gradients project visual heatmaps highlight pixel matrices which were central to the classification outputs.

Step 05

Dermatologist Review

A certified clinician cross-checks the diagnostic results, validates the heatmap maps, and adds verified observations.

Step 06

Certified Reporting

The final findings, metrics, heatmaps, and doctor observations are compiled into an official PDF report with disclaimer standards.
